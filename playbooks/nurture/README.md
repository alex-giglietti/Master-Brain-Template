# Nurture Playbooks

Follow-up sequences, relationship building, and retention strategies.

## Purpose

The Nurture stage maintains relationships with leads not yet ready to buy and keeps existing customers engaged and satisfied. Playbooks here ensure no opportunity falls through the cracks.

## Bot Behavior in Nurture Stage

When nurturing relationships, the bot should:

1. **Provide value first** - Every interaction should help them
2. **Remember context** - Reference past conversations
3. **Be patient** - Don't push for sales prematurely
4. **Stay top of mind** - Regular, relevant touchpoints
5. **Identify buying signals** - Route back to Convert when ready

## Nurture Cadence by Lead Temperature

| Temperature | Touchpoint Frequency | Channel |
|-------------|---------------------|---------|
| Hot | Every 2-3 days | Direct message/call |
| Warm | Weekly | Email + occasional DM |
| Cool | Bi-weekly | Email |
| Cold | Monthly | Email newsletter |

## Re-engagement Triggers

When to reach out to dormant leads:
- New product/feature launch
- Industry news relevant to them
- Seasonal/annual milestones
- Case study from similar company
- Special promotion

## Metrics to Track

- Email open/click rates
- Response rates
- Re-engagement success
- Customer lifetime value
- Net Promoter Score
