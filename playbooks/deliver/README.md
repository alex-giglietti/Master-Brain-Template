# Deliver Playbooks

Onboarding, service delivery, and customer success protocols.

## Purpose

The Deliver stage ensures customers get value from their purchase and become advocates. Playbooks here standardize excellent service delivery.

## Key Playbooks to Create

### 1. `onboarding.md`
New customer onboarding process.
- Welcome sequence
- Setup checklist
- Training schedule
- Success milestones

### 2. `project-kickoff.md`
Starting new projects with clients.
- Kickoff meeting agenda
- Expectation setting
- Communication protocols
- Timeline review

### 3. `support-triage.md`
Handling support requests efficiently.
- Issue categorization
- Priority levels
- Response templates
- Escalation paths

### 4. `success-milestones.md`
Celebrating and tracking customer success.
- Milestone definitions
- Communication templates
- Case study requests

### 5. `offboarding.md`
Graceful customer exits (when applicable).
- Exit interview
- Knowledge transfer
- Final deliverables
- Re-engagement options

## Bot Behavior in Deliver Stage

When supporting customers, the bot should:

1. **Be responsive** - Fast initial response, even if just acknowledgment
2. **Set expectations** - Clear timelines for resolution
3. **Follow procedures** - Consistent delivery process
4. **Document everything** - Log interactions for team visibility
5. **Celebrate wins** - Acknowledge customer success

## Support Triage Matrix

| Issue Type | Priority | Response Time | Owner |
|------------|----------|---------------|-------|
| System down | Critical | 15 minutes | On-call |
| Feature broken | High | 2 hours | Support |
| Question | Medium | 24 hours | Bot/Support |
| Feature request | Low | 1 week | Product |

## Onboarding Success Checklist

- [ ] Welcome email sent
- [ ] Kickoff call scheduled
- [ ] Account/access created
- [ ] Training completed
- [ ] First milestone achieved
- [ ] Check-in call completed
- [ ] Feedback collected

## Customer Health Indicators

**Healthy:**
- Regular engagement
- Responding to outreach
- Using product/service fully
- Providing referrals

**At Risk:**
- Declining engagement
- Support tickets increasing
- Slow to respond
- Negative feedback

## Metrics to Track

- Time to first value
- Onboarding completion rate
- Support ticket volume
- Customer satisfaction (CSAT)
- Customer retention rate
