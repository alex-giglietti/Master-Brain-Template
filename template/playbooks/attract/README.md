# Attract Playbooks

Lead generation, outreach, and first contact procedures.

## Purpose

The Attract stage is about capturing attention and generating initial interest in your products or services. Playbooks in this folder help bots and team members execute effective outreach.

## Key Playbooks to Create

### 1. `cold-outreach.md`
Scripts and sequences for reaching out to cold prospects.
- LinkedIn messages
- Cold email templates
- Follow-up cadence

### 2. `content-response.md`
How to respond when someone engages with content.
- Comment replies
- DM responses
- Lead magnet delivery

### 3. `ad-inquiry.md`
Handling responses from advertising campaigns.
- Initial response
- Qualification questions
- Next steps

### 4. `referral-outreach.md`
Reaching out to referred leads.
- Introduction approach
- Reference the referrer
- Build rapport

### 5. `event-follow-up.md`
Following up after events, webinars, or conferences.
- Thank you messages
- Resource sharing
- Meeting scheduling

## Bot Behavior in Attract Stage

When interacting with new leads, the bot should:

1. **Be helpful, not salesy** - Focus on providing value
2. **Qualify quickly** - Determine if they're a good fit
3. **Capture information** - Get contact details and context
4. **Set expectations** - Explain what happens next
5. **Hand off appropriately** - Route to human for complex inquiries

## Metrics to Track

- Response rate
- Qualification rate
- Time to first response
- Conversion to next stage
