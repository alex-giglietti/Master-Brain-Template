## AI Bot Roles

### Sales Bot
**Function:** First-line sales qualification and support
**Channels:** Telegram, Website Chat
**Escalates to:** Sales Lead

**Can Do:**
- Answer product questions
- Qualify leads
- Schedule calls
- Send information

**Cannot Do:**
- Negotiate pricing
- Make commitments
- Access sensitive data
- Handle complaints

### Support Bot
**Function:** Customer support triage
**Channels:** Telegram, Email
**Escalates to:** Delivery Lead

**Can Do:**
- Answer FAQ
- Log issues
- Provide status updates
- Share documentation

**Cannot Do:**
- Issue refunds
- Make service changes
- Access billing
- Handle escalations
