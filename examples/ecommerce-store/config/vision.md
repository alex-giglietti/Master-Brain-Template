# GreenLeaf Co. - Company Overview

## Who We Are
GreenLeaf Co. is a direct-to-consumer sustainable home goods brand. We sell eco-friendly cleaning products, reusable household items, and zero-waste starter kits through our online store and select retail partners.

## Our Mission
Make sustainable living accessible and affordable for every household, one product swap at a time.

## Our Vision
A world where choosing the eco-friendly option is the easiest and most affordable choice for consumers.

## Core Values
1. **Sustainability First** - Every business decision considers environmental impact
2. **Radical Transparency** - Full ingredient lists, supply chain visibility, honest marketing
3. **Community Over Competition** - We collaborate with other eco-brands and educate freely

## Target Market
- Eco-conscious consumers aged 25-45
- Household income $60K-$150K
- Urban and suburban, US-based
- Already making some sustainable choices
- Looking for convenient, affordable options

## Unique Value Proposition
Premium eco-friendly home products at everyday prices, with a subscription model that saves customers 20% and ensures they never run out of essentials.
