# Abandoned Cart Recovery Playbook

## Purpose
Re-engage customers who added items to their cart but didn't complete checkout.

## When to Use
- Cart abandoned for 1+ hours
- Customer has email on file (account or entered at checkout)
- Cart value is $15+

## Recovery Sequence

### Email 1: Gentle Reminder (1 hour after abandonment)
**Subject:** You left something behind!

Hi [FirstName],

Looks like you were checking out some of our eco-friendly goodies but didn't finish. Your cart is still saved:

[Cart items with images]

Complete your order and start making a difference today.

[Complete My Order - Button]

Questions? Just reply to this email.

### Email 2: Value Add (24 hours)
**Subject:** Why customers love [Product Name]

Hi [FirstName],

Still thinking about it? Here's what other customers say about [top item in cart]:

"[Customer review quote]" - [Reviewer Name]

Plus, every purchase:
- Eliminates [X] single-use plastic items
- Ships in 100% recyclable packaging
- Includes a free sustainability tip card

[Complete My Order - Button]

### Email 3: Incentive (48 hours)
**Subject:** 10% off - just for you

Hi [FirstName],

We'd love to help you take the first step toward a greener home. Here's 10% off your cart:

Code: **GREENSTART10**

[Cart items]
Original: $[X]
With discount: $[Y]

This code expires in 48 hours.

[Use My Discount - Button]

## Bot Response (if customer messages)
If a customer with an abandoned cart reaches out via chat:

1. Acknowledge their interest
2. Ask if they have questions about specific products
3. Offer to help with product selection
4. If hesitant on price, mention the subscription discount (20% off)
5. Do NOT proactively offer the discount code - save that for email sequence

## Success Metrics
- Recovery rate target: 15-20%
- Average recovered cart value: $45+
- Time to recovery: Under 48 hours

## Escalation
- If customer reports technical issues with checkout -> Forward to support team
- If cart value is $100+ and customer is engaged -> Offer free shipping instead of %
