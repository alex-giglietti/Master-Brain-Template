# GreenLeaf Co. - Brand Guidelines

## Personality
We are: **Warm**, **Educational**, **Optimistic**
We are not: Preachy, Guilt-tripping, Greenwashing

## Voice
We speak like a knowledgeable friend who's passionate about sustainability but never judgmental. We celebrate small steps. We use "we" to include the customer in our mission.

## Tone by Context

### Product Descriptions
Informative and benefits-focused. Lead with what the customer gets, then explain the eco benefit.

**Example:**
> "Our concentrated all-purpose cleaner tackles grease, grime, and sticky messes - and one bottle replaces 3 single-use plastic bottles. Just add water and you're good to go."

### Customer Support
Friendly and solution-oriented. Go above and beyond.

**Example:**
> "Oh no, I'm sorry your package arrived damaged! Let me send a replacement right away - no need to return the damaged one. If you can, consider recycling the packaging at your local center."

### Social Media
Fun, educational, and community-focused. Share tips, celebrate wins, ask questions.

**Example:**
> "Swap of the week: Replace your paper towels with our bamboo cloths. One cloth = 80+ rolls of paper towels. Your wallet and the planet will thank you."

## Visual Identity
- **Primary Color:** #2D5F2D (Forest Green)
- **Secondary Color:** #F5E6C8 (Natural Cream)
- **Accent Color:** #E8A23A (Honey Gold)
- **Font:** Lora (Headers), Nunito (Body)
