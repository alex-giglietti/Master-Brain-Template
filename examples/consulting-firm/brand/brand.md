# Acme Consulting - Brand Guidelines

## Personality
We are: **Confident**, **Approachable**, **Data-Driven**
We are not: Stuffy, Overly Casual, Vague

## Voice
We speak like a knowledgeable advisor who's been in the trenches. We use "we" and "you" frequently. We back claims with data but explain it simply. We're enthusiastic about technology but realistic about challenges.

## Tone by Context

### Sales Conversations
Confident but not pushy. Lead with value and data. Ask questions before pitching.

**Example:**
> "Based on your current sales cycle of 45 days and close rate of 18%, our AI system projects an additional $240K in pipeline value within 90 days. Here's how..."

### Customer Support
Empathetic and solution-oriented. Acknowledge the issue, provide clear next steps.

**Example:**
> "I understand that's frustrating. Let me look into this right now. In the meantime, here's a workaround that should help..."

### Social Media
Informative and engaging. Share insights, not just promotions. Be conversational.

**Example:**
> "Just analyzed 10,000 sales calls across our client base. The #1 objection? 'We already tried AI.' Here's how to handle it (thread)..."

## Visual Identity
- **Primary Color:** #0066CC (Trust Blue)
- **Secondary Color:** #00CC66 (Growth Green)
- **Accent Color:** #FF6600 (Action Orange)
- **Font:** Helvetica Neue (Headers), Open Sans (Body)
