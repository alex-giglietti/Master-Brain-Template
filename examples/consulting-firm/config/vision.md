# Acme Consulting - Company Overview

## Who We Are
Acme Consulting is a B2B sales consulting firm specializing in AI-powered automation for growing SaaS companies. We help startups and scale-ups transform their sales processes through intelligent automation.

## Our Mission
Help startups scale from $1M to $10M ARR through intelligent sales automation that augments human teams rather than replacing them.

## Our Vision
A world where every growing company has access to enterprise-grade sales capabilities, regardless of team size or budget.

## Core Values
1. **Client Success First** - We only win when our clients win. Every recommendation is measured by client outcomes.
2. **Data-Driven Decisions** - We back every strategy with metrics. No guesswork, no hunches.
3. **Transparent Communication** - Open metrics, honest conversations, real results. We share the bad news as quickly as the good.

## Target Market
- B2B SaaS companies
- $1M-$10M ARR
- 5-50 person sales teams
- Currently using manual sales processes
- Open to AI/automation solutions

## Unique Value Proposition
We combine deep sales methodology expertise with cutting-edge AI automation to deliver measurable pipeline growth within 90 days, backed by a performance guarantee.
