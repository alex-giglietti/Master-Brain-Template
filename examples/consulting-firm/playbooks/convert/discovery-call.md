# Discovery Call Playbook

## Purpose
Qualify prospects and understand their current sales process to determine fit for our AI automation solutions.

## When to Use
- After initial outreach generates interest
- When a lead books a discovery call
- When inbound leads request a consultation

## Pre-Call Preparation
1. Review the prospect's LinkedIn profile and company website
2. Check their current tech stack (BuiltWith, LinkedIn, etc.)
3. Note their company size, industry, and recent news
4. Prepare 2-3 personalized questions based on research

## Call Structure (30 minutes)

### Opening (5 min)
"Thanks for taking the time today, [Name]. Before we dive in, I'd love to understand your current situation so I can make sure this conversation is valuable for you. Sound good?"

### Discovery Questions (15 min)
1. "Walk me through your current sales process from lead to close."
2. "What's your average sales cycle length right now?"
3. "What does your team spend the most time on that isn't directly closing deals?"
4. "Have you tried any automation or AI tools before? What was that experience like?"
5. "If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing about your sales process, what would it be?"

### Present Solution (5 min)
Only if they qualify:
- Connect their specific pain points to our capabilities
- Share 1-2 relevant case studies
- Outline expected ROI based on their numbers

### Next Steps (5 min)
- If qualified: Book a demo/proposal presentation
- If not ready: Add to nurture sequence
- If not a fit: Refer to partner or resource

## Qualification Criteria
Must meet at least 3 of 5:
- [ ] B2B SaaS or professional services
- [ ] 5+ person sales team
- [ ] $1M+ ARR
- [ ] Currently manual sales process
- [ ] Budget of $5K+/month for tools

## Escalation
Hand off to founder/CEO if:
- Enterprise prospect ($10M+ ARR)
- Requests custom pricing
- Technical deep-dive needed
