# START HERE
## Your AI Marketing Machine — Quick Start Guide

Welcome to your AI Brain. This folder is the knowledge base that powers your AI Chief of Staff. Everything your AI needs to know about your business, your marketing, and how to execute lives here.

---

## How This Works

Your AI assistant reads these files to understand:
- **Who you are** (Vision & Brand folders)
- **How to execute** (Playbooks & Factory folders)
- **What's happening** (Memory folder)

When you ask your AI to "build me a funnel" or "write an email sequence," it references these files to do the work correctly — in YOUR voice, for YOUR audience.

## How to Use Your AI

### Asking for Marketing Tasks

Once your Vision and Brand are complete, you can ask things like:

- "Build me a lead magnet funnel for [offer]"
- "Write a 5-email nurture sequence for new leads"
- "Create 3 Facebook ad variations for my challenge"
- "Set up a book-a-call funnel with an application"
- "Write this week's content based on my pillars"

Your AI will reference the playbooks and your brand docs to execute.

### Asking for Operations Tasks

- "What's on my calendar this week?"
- "Create a project for [initiative]"
- "Send a follow-up to [contact]"
- "What tasks are overdue?"
- "Draft a report on this month's leads"

## The Memory Folder

This helps your AI remember context across conversations. You don't need to manage this — it happens automatically.
